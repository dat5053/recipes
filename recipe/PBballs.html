Peanut Butter Energy Balls

Description

My kids favorite peanut butter oatmeal energy balls. A great Healthy snack!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/3 cup peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Ground Flaxseed
  • optional add ins (chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, sprinkles)

    • Steps

    1. Grind oats to desired consistency.
    2. Add all ingredients together into a bowl and mix well.
    3. Scoop up a bit with a spoon and roll it into ball. Continue with the rest of the mix.
    4. Store in an airtight container for up to a week.
