Baked Ziti

Description

This is my favorite baked ziti recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 box Ziti
  • 1lb ground sausage
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1tbsp Olive Oil
  • As much garlic as you would like
  • 1.5 jars marinara sauce
  • 3 Cups mozzarella
  • 1 cup ricotta
  • italian seasoning to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350
  2. Boil the ziti following the directions on the package.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan on medium heat. Throw in the diced onion and garlic. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Throw the ground sausage in the pan and cook till browned.
  5. While the sausage is cooking mix ricotta, half the mozzarella, salt and pepper, and italian seasoning. Then add the sauce and mix till combined.
  6. When everything is finished cooking combine everything in a baking dish and mix well.
  7. Top with remianing mozzarella and a sprinkle of parmesan.
  8. Bake till cheese is crispy and melted and sauce is bubbling, about 30 minutes.
  9. Serve and Enjoy!
